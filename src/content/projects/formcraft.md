FormCraft is a ground-up redesign of GitHub's support contact forms. The goal: make it effortless for customers to describe their problem and get routed to the right team.

Every design decision is informed by agent feedback — the people who see the downstream impact of form choices. We've established CSAT baselines and are driving measurable improvements through iterative design.
