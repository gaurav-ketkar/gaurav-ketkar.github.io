Project Nexus is GitHub's AI-powered ticket automation system. It uses language models to classify incoming support tickets, route them to the right team, and — increasingly — resolve common queries without human intervention.

As the product lead, I own the roadmap, define success metrics, and make trade-off decisions between automation coverage and accuracy. Key decisions have included bot persona design, per-category metric toggles, and the strategic sequencing of which ticket categories to automate first.
