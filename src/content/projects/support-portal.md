The Support Portal project is a cross-divisional effort to improve how GitHub's partners interact with support systems. It spans multiple environments and requires close coordination between Customer Success Engineering, Support operations, and Partner-facing teams.

My role involves navigating organizational complexity, aligning stakeholders, and ensuring the technical implementation serves both partner administrators and their end-users.
